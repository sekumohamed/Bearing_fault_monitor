ABOUT & TRAINING RESULTS
Dataset: CWRU Bearing Dataset, 12kHz Drive End sensor, 1hp load condition, 10 classes (Normal + 3 fault types × 3 severities).
Preprocessing: signals windowed at 1024 samples (512 stride, 50% overlap), z-score normalized per window. Train/test split performed on the raw signal before windowing to prevent data leakage between overlapping windows.
Model: lightweight 1D-CNN, 3 convolutional blocks + global average pooling + fully-connected classifier, ~15K trainable parameters.
Optimizer study: identical architecture, weight initialization, and data across three SGD variants (Standard, Momentum, Nesterov) to isolate optimizer effects.
